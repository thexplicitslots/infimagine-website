How it works

A clear path from spark to solid.

You do not need a finished CAD file. A sketch, reference image, rough dimensions, or voice note is enough to start shaping the idea.

  1. 01

    Share the idea

    Send the concept, purpose, size, finish, and any reference files.

  2. 02

    Shape the design

    We check feasibility, suggest materials, and prepare the print plan.

  3. 03

    Print and refine

    The object is printed, cleaned, tested, and finished for its use case.

  4. 04

    Hand over reality

    You receive the final piece with the option to iterate or reorder.
