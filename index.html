How it works

A clear path from spark to solid.

You do not need a finished CAD file. A sketch, reference image, rough dimension, or voice note is enough to begin engineering the object.

  1. Share the idea

    Send the concept, purpose, dimensions, finish direction, and references.

  2. Shape the design

    We assess feasibility, suggest materials, and prepare a clean print plan.

  3. Print and refine

    The object is printed, cleaned, tested, and finished for its real use case.

  4. Hand over reality

    You receive the final piece with a path to iterate, improve, or reorder.
